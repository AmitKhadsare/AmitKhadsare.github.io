Our Care Services

We offer comprehensive home care services tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual and family.

Personal Care

Assistance with daily activities including bathing, dressing, grooming, and medication management.

  • Bathing and hygiene assistance
  • Medication reminders
  • Mobility support

Companion Care

Social interaction, emotional support, and companionship to enhance quality of life.

  • Conversation and social interaction
  • Activity planning and engagement
  • Transportation assistance

Meal Preparation

Nutritious meal planning, preparation, and assistance with eating when needed.

  • Custom meal planning
  • Grocery shopping
  • Special dietary needs

Light Housekeeping

Maintaining a clean, safe, and comfortable living environment for our clients.

  • Light cleaning and tidying
  • Laundry assistance
  • Organization and maintenance

Health Monitoring

Regular health checks and coordination with healthcare providers for optimal wellness.

  • Vital signs monitoring
  • Appointment scheduling
  • Health record maintenance

24/7 Care

Round-the-clock care services for clients requiring continuous support and monitoring.

  • Live-in care options
  • Emergency response
  • Family communication

About Columbia Care Home

For over 15 years, Columbia Care Home has been a trusted name in home care services. We understand that aging in place is not just a preference—it's about maintaining dignity, independence, and connection to the memories that make a house a home.
