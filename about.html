About Columbia Care Home

For over 15 years, we've been dedicated to providing compassionate, professional home care services that help families stay together in the comfort of their own homes.

Our Story

Columbia Care Home was founded in 2009 with a simple but powerful mission: to provide exceptional home care services that allow individuals to age gracefully in the comfort and familiarity of their own homes.

Our founder, Sarah Mitchell, RN, witnessed firsthand the challenges families face when trying to balance caring for aging loved ones while maintaining their own lives. She recognized that with the right support, most people could continue living independently at home rather than moving to institutional care.

Today, we've grown to serve hundreds of families throughout Columbia County and surrounding areas, but our commitment to personalized, compassionate care remains unchanged.

Our Mission & Values

Everything we do is guided by our core mission and fundamental values that put our clients and their families first.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, professional home care services that enable individuals to maintain their independence, dignity, and quality of life while remaining in the comfort of their own homes surrounded by the people and memories they cherish most.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected home care provider in our community, known for our unwavering commitment to excellence, compassion, and the belief that everyone deserves to age with grace and dignity in their own home.

Compassion

We treat every client with genuine care, empathy, and respect, understanding that each person has unique needs and preferences.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our interactions, being honest, transparent, and reliable in everything we do.

Excellence

We continuously strive to exceed expectations through ongoing training, innovation, and commitment to quality care.

Partnership

We work closely with families, healthcare providers, and community resources to ensure comprehensive, coordinated care.
